Pooled Shipping

Your parcel travels with other buyers heading to the same destination. Lower cost, same secure handling. Best for non-urgent orders.

  • Lowest shipping rates
  • Weekly departures
  • Fully tracked & insured
  • 5–7 days to Harare
+ Small fee
Priority · Fastest route

Express Shipping

Your package gets its own dedicated run. Priority handling, faster border clearance, quicker delivery. Ideal when you can't wait.

  • Dedicated express run
  • Faster border processing
  • Real-time WhatsApp updates
  • 2–3 days to Harare
